About Me

Food strategist.
Storyteller.
Obsessed with food.

I'm Talia — a content strategist and food storyteller based in New York City. I specialize in short-form video and social strategy for restaurants and food brands that know their product is exceptional and need their content to say the same.

Right now, I lead digital content for Golden Steer NYC, translating 65 years of steakhouse history into content that feels alive, culturally relevant, and worth sharing. Before that, I rebuilt Good Bird Restaurant's social presence in New Orleans — growing brand awareness by over 1,000% in two months from the ground up.

My foundation is a little different from most strategists in this space. I studied Marketing at Tulane University, where I graduated cum laude, and then went deeper — completing a Master's in Food Studies at NYU. I understand how people buy, and I understand food on a level that most marketers don't. This helps me shape my marketing strategy with a different eye.

Outside of client work, I stay sharp through running, reading, and cooking.

I won't chase a trending sound if it doesn't serve the brand. I won't post just to hit a quota. Every piece I create has a clear purpose tied to a real goal — and if it doesn't, I say so. That honesty is part of the work.

Good Bird Restaurant
Restaurant · Social Rebuild · Content Production · Influencer Outreach
+
The Challenge

Good Bird was a New Orleans restaurant with a strong in-room experience and a flat online presence. Their social channels weren't reflecting what made the place worth visiting and in a city where food culture is its own religion, that's a competitive liability.

The Approach

I rebuilt their social presence from the ground up, original food photography, short-form video content, and a brand voice that matched Good Bird's energy: warm, local, unpretentious, proud. I implemented a seasonal menu campaign strategy and launched a local food creator outreach program to build grassroots awareness.

The Results

Within the first two months: brand awareness grew by +1,019% and total followers grew 200+. The content strategy shifted the account from dormant to driving real-world discovery and reservations.

03
Golden Steer Seasonings
CPG · E-Commerce · UGC Strategy · Influencer Marketing · Paid Social
+
The Challenge

Golden Steer has a robust ecommerce line focused on seasonings, steaks, and butters — a product backed by 65 years of steakhouse credibility. But the marketing wasn't matching the quality of what was being created. There was no cohesive content strategy, no established visual identity for the product line, or a content strategy to move people along the awareness funnel. The business needed content that would align with the overall brand and would both stop the scroll and convert.

The Results

Over 5 months, built a full content ecosystem for the seasoning line. Helped run a recurring creator program with 20–30 influencers per month — 200+ creator touchpoints total — generating a consistent pipeline of authentic, brand-aligned organic and UGC content. Sourced and managed product seeding end-to-end. Turned top-performing UGC into targeted Facebook ad creative, developing individual ad videos for each seasoning SKU. Shot original product photography to anchor the visual identity. Aligned all content with the broader Golden Steer brand aesthetic, creating a cohesive look and feel across organic and paid.
